**Chip** — tappable rounded pill, typically in a horizontal scroll row of suggested actions above the composer.

```jsx
<Chip icon={<ArrowRightIcon/>} onClick={fill}>Remove silences</Chip>
<Chip icon={<ArrowRightIcon/>}>Tighten pacing</Chip>
```

- Rests on `--surface-1`; hover fills `--accent-wash` with an accent-tinted border and the leading arrow in emerald.
