# P3.3 — RightPanes header Help popovers

**Seed:** `tests/e2e/seed.spec.ts`
**Roadmap status:** ✅ done (`76d78db`)

## Application Overview

Each right-side pane (Background, Layout, Video effects, Cursor, Timeline, Transcript) has a `HelpCircle` icon in its header. Clicking the icon should open a small popover with **contextual help text** describing what the pane does. The popover should be dismissable (click the icon again, click outside, or press Esc).

## Test Scenarios

### 1. Clicking Help opens a popover with text

**Setup**

1. Open the editor at `http://localhost:5173/?windowType=editor`.
2. Wait for the right-side panes to mount.

**Steps**

1. Click the `HelpCircle` icon in the **Background** pane header.

**Expected results**

- A popover appears below the header.
- The popover contains the help text specific to the Background pane (mentions wallpaper, solid color, gradient, custom image — anything that matches the configured `helpText` prop).
- The popover is visible, on top of the layer below (`z-index` ≥ 50).
- The `HelpCircle` button reports `aria-expanded="true"`.

### 2. Clicking Help again closes the popover

**Setup**

1. Popover from scenario 1 is open.

**Steps**

1. Click the `HelpCircle` icon again.

**Expected results**

- The popover disappears.
- The `HelpCircle` button reports `aria-expanded="false"`.

### 3. Help text is different per pane

**Setup**

1. Open the editor.

**Steps**

1. Open the Background pane help — record the text.
2. Open the Layout pane help — record the text.
3. Open the Video effects pane help — record the text.

**Expected results**

- The three recorded help texts are not all identical. Each pane shows pane-specific guidance, not a generic "Settings for X" fallback.
